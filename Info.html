INSTRUCTIONS :-

  • Add Tickets Using '+' button in the NavBar
  • The Modal that appears after clicking '+' gives you option to write your task and also choose the color according to the priority of the task
    • Choose 'Pink' for the task having low priority
    • Choose 'Blue' for the task having Medium priority
    • Choose 'Green' for the task having high priority
    • Choose 'Red' for the task which is extremely Urgent
  • After entering the task in the modal. Press Enter to generate the ticket corresponding to that task
  • In order to give a line gap while entering the task in the modal, press 'Shift + Enter'
  • The tickets generated are editable
    • Priority color can be changed by simply clicking on it.
    • In order to edit the task, unlock the ticket first by clicking on the 'lock' icon
  • In order to delete a particular ticket :-
    • Click on the 'x' icon inside the NavBar
    • After that, click on the ticket that you desire to delete
    • After deleting the required ticket(s), again click on the 'delete icon'
  • In order to delete all the tickets at once, then click on 'delete' icon inside the NavBar
  • In order to view only the tickets having a particular priority color, click on the corresponding color inside the NavBar
  • All the tasks are locally stored inside the browser
  • Tickets once deleted cannot be restored
